About me

I'm a formal semanticist, which means that my work involves developing models of how we slot meanings together to determine the information conveyed by a sentence, using tools from logic and computer science. I'm currently a visiting faculty member in the linguistics & philosophy department at MIT. Reach me at patrick.d.elliott@gmail.com.
